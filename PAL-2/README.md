# PAL-2  
Two IO Board Projects for the PAL-2 System.  
Similar features, different Layout.  
  
RS232, PA0-7 , PB0-7 (incl. PB6) and DecEn IO Pins,  
TTY & KBD Switch,  
RS232 & USB Serial Switch,  
Straight & Nullmopdem Switch,  
Power Switch.
